Giving Concessions

Gonzaga over the years has acknowledged the need for change and has even combated this with programs such as their installation of solar panels atop Cantwell Reusch. Gonzaga has also supported clubs such as Eagles for Water and the Sustainability Club. However, there is still many gaping issues they have yet to address, but luckily we have solutions.

Light Sensors

Have you ever noticed how despite most classrooms having very large windows, overflowing with natural light, the lights are still on? This is a common issue at Gonzaga, and it is one of the many reasons why we are consuming so much power. A solution to this we propose is the installation of light sensors. These sensors would be able to detect the amount of light in a room and activate the light according the need.

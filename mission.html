We are dedicated to making Gonzaga more sustainable and environmentally friendly.

What is our mission?

For Hack Eye Street we are dedicated to making Gonzaga a much more sustainable campus. We are working to reduce the amount of energy that we use and to make sure that we are using it in the most efficient way possible. We are also working to make sure that we are using the most sustainable energy sources possible like solar and wind.
